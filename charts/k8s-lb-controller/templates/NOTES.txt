1. Confirm the controller is running:
   kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }},app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "k8s-lb-controller.name" . }}

2. Example managed Service:

   apiVersion: v1
   kind: Service
   metadata:
     name: demo
   spec:
     type: LoadBalancer
     loadBalancerClass: {{ .Values.controller.loadBalancerClass }}
     selector:
       app: demo
     ports:
       - port: 80
         targetPort: 80

3. Watch the assigned external address:
   kubectl get svc demo -o wide
